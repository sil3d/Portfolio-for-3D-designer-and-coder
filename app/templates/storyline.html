The Spark

Every journey begins with a spark. For me, it was the realization that I could bring my imagination to life through 3D design and engineering. I started with simple shapes, learning the fundamentals of CAD.

Rapid Growth

As my skills sharpened, so did my ambition. I moved from static models to complex assemblies, integrating electronics and simulations. Hours turned into days of refining every vertex and line.

Innovation & Impact

Today, my focus is on innovation that matters. Building autonomous systems, drones, and AI-driven solutions. It's not just about making things look good it's about making them work beautifully.
